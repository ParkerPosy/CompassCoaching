The purpose of this document is to outline the envisioned process for coaching individuals looking for help outlining their future. Broadly, our assistance will include three separate steps. First, determine the desired outcome. Why are they here, what goals or aspirations do they have, Etc. Second, is process – how can we achieve those goals. Figure out what potential challenges lie ahead and how to overcome them. Third, is to achieve the goals and have the person hold themselves accountable. In this stage we are in the process of achieving those goals.
The vision is to help people at each stage of the process by providing resources that are not available to everyone. Particularly resources of knowledge. For Example – Help budgeting, help writing a resume, cover letter, or application essays, and from the beginning helping them to find out what they even want to do.
